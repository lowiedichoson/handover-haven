# 🚀 Handover Haven

Welcome to the **official team handover & turnover knowledge base** — a living
document where departing team members leave behind everything the next person
needs to succeed.

## Who Is This For?

- **You, leaving:** Document your responsibilities, ongoing work, gotchas, and
  unwritten knowledge before you go.
- **You, arriving:** Onboard faster by reading the handovers of those who came
  before you.
- **You, staying:** Understand the full scope of what a departing teammate owned.

## Philosophy

> "Don't let knowledge walk out the door."

Every resignation is a risk. This project exists to turn that risk into a
**structured, reusable asset** — not just a zip of files or a frantic 30-minute
call.
