# Handovers

This is where the knowledge lives. Every person who moved on left behind a
structured handover — systems they owned, requests they handled, gotchas they
discovered, and the context you won't find in a README.

## How Handovers Are Organized

Each person gets their own folder under `docs/handovers/<name>/`. Inside, you'll
find a consistent layout:

- **About Me** — role, tenure, team, and parting notes
- **Systems** — servers, apps, repos, and infrastructure they owned
- **Common Requests** — recurring tasks, runbooks, and how-tos
- **Databases** — schemas, key tables, and data flows

For business context on each company (what they do, which systems they run),
see the [Companies](../companies/index.md) section.
