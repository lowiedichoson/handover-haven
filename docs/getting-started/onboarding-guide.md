# Onboarding Guide

New to the team? This guide tells you what to read first based on your role.

## If You're Joining the Development Team (or Replacing Someone Who Recently Resigned)

Start here — this covers what you'll need:

1. [About](../about.md) — why this knowledge base exists
2. Browse the [**Handovers**](../handovers/introduction.md) section — read the handover of the person you're replacing (or the closest role to yours) to understand their scope, the systems they owned, and common requests they handled
3. [Glossary](glossary.md) — decode the acronyms before your first meeting

Then read the company pages relevant to your systems:
- PEMI if you're working on Clientease or LOI Generator
- VFC if you're working on eSettlement, eTerminal, or Navision
- VEI if you're maintaining the legacy Vantage website

## If You're a New ICTG Team Member

1. [About](../about.md) — why this knowledge base exists
2. [Glossary](glossary.md) — learn the language
3. [Handovers Introduction](../handovers/introduction.md) — see who's documented what
4. Skim the systems list under each person's handover to understand the landscape

## If You're a Manager

1. [Handovers Introduction](../handovers/introduction.md) — who's leaving, who's staying
2. The handover of the person reporting to you — understand their scope
3. [How to Use](how-to-use.md) — so you can guide your reports through the process
4. [Template](template.md) — review what your team should be filling out

## General Advice

- **Read the handover of the person you're replacing first**, then branch out.
- **Don't try to memorize everything.** Use this as a reference — come back when you need it.
- **If you find something wrong or outdated, fix it.** This is a living document.
