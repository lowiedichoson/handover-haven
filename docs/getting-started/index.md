# Getting Started

Welcome! If you're new here — whether you're about to resign or just exploring —
this section will get you up to speed.

## Quick Start

1. Read the [How to Use](how-to-use.md) guide to understand the workflow.
2. Copy the [Template](template.md) and fill it in with your knowledge.
3. Add your handover under `docs/handovers/<your-name>/` and update `mkdocs.yml`.
4. Open a PR. That's it.

If you're new to the team, start with the [Onboarding Guide](onboarding-guide.md)
instead — it tells you what to read based on your role. Stuck on an acronym?
Check the [Glossary](glossary.md).

## Who Should Contribute

- **Resigning team members** — your handover is your responsibility.
- **Managers** — ensure departing team members complete their handover.
- **Anyone** — if you discover outdated or missing info, fix it.
